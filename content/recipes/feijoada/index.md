**Ingredients**
*Serves 4*

- 1 can black beans (15.5 oz)
- 1 lb pork tenderloin
- 1 lb pork sausages (preferably smoked)
- 1 package bacon
- 1 garlic
- 1 onion
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 Tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 cup water

**Directions**

1. Wash black beans
1. Cube tenderloin, slice sausages, bacon, and chop onion.
1. Brown meat, then add all ingredients into slow cooker
1. Serve with brown rice, collared greens
