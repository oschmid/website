**Ingredients**
*Serves 1*

- 2 cups frozen berries (blueberries, blackberries, strawberries) or other fruits
- 2 Tbsp Holy Crap seed mix (or chia, flax, hemp)
- 2 Tbsp cashew or other nut milk
- 1 handful of nuts (macadamia, walnuts, pecans, almonds)
- 1/2 cup yogurt (optional)

**Directions**

1. Put frozen fruit in a bowl and microwave on power level 3/10 for 8 mins.
1. While fruit is defrosting, soak seed mix in nut milk.
1. Stir soaked seeds into fruits, top with nuts.
