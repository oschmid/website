**Ingredients**

- 4 packages of gelatine
- 4 cups water
- 1 cups sugar
- 500 ml. mango pulp (1 can)
- 500 ml. whipping cream

**Directions**

1. Prepare gelatine according to package direction
1. Boil water and add sugar and gelatine
1. Wait until liquid cools
1. Mix in whipping cream and mango pulp
1. Pour into containers
1. Add other cut up fruits if you like
1. Refrigerate
