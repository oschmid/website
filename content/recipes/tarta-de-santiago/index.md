**Ingredients**
*Serves 6-8*

- 500g almond flour
- 275g sugar
- 6 eggs separated
- Grated zest of 1 lemon
- Grated zest of 1 orange
- 4 drops almond extract
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)
- Confectioners sugar for dusting

**Directions**

1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9-inch (23cm) round cake pan and line the bottom with parchment paper.
1. Beat egg yolks with sugar to a smooth pale cream. Beat in the zests and almond extract. Fold in the almond flour and cinnamon (if using) until well combined.
1. In a separate bowl and with clean beater, beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form. Gently fold the egg whites into the almond mixture until just combined.
1. Grease an 11-inch springform pan, with butter and dust it with flour. Pour in the cake batter, and bake the cake for 40 mins or until it feels firm to the touch. Let cool before turning out.
1. Just before serving, dust the top of the cake with confectioners' sugar. Traditionally, a cross of Saint James is placed on top of the cake before dusting with sugar, and then removed to leave a cross-shaped pattern.
